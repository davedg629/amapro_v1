EasyAMA is one of a few apps created by GroupSRC. We've also created the following apps that make Reddit a little easier to use.

rDrafts

rDrafts is our most popular app. It allows you to create a draft of a Reddit post, preview it, share the preview with others, and then submit it at your convenience (no scheduling though).

Visit rdrafts.gropusrc.com

EasyUnsub

EasyUnsub is for redditors that want to quickly unsubscribe from multiple subreddits. A common use-case is when you have created a new account and want to quickly unsubscribe from the default subreddits..

Visit easyunsub.groupsrc.com
